About Us

Who We Are

7risten.ca is a boutique website design and development company based in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan. We specialize in creating custom, high-performance websites for small businesses.

What We Do

We provide comprehensive website design, development, and maintenance services with a focus on affordability and value for small businesses.

Why We Do It

Our mission is to create beautiful, high-performance websites that deliver exceptional user experiences. We are passionate about helping small businesses establish a strong online presence through visually stunning, secure, and fast websites.

Founded in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan, our team is dedicated to excellence in web development. We pride ourselves on our meticulous attention to detail and our commitment to client satisfaction.

Our values

Transparency, innovation, and integrity, which guide us in every project we undertake.

Our Team

Tristen MacPherson is the driving force behind 7risten.ca. He discovered his passion for web development while earning his B.A. Sc. in Computer Science at the University of Saskatchewan.

Services

We offer a comprehensive range of services to meet your web development needs:
